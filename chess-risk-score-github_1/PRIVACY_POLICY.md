# Chess Risk Score — Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** March 11, 2026

## Overview

Chess Risk Score is a browser extension that analyses publicly available chess data to generate opponent risk assessments on Chess.com and Lichess. We are committed to protecting your privacy.

## Data Collection

**Chess Risk Score does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.**

Specifically:

- **No personal information** is collected from users
- **No browsing data** is tracked or recorded
- **No analytics or telemetry** data is sent anywhere
- **No cookies** are set by the extension
- **No accounts** are created or required
- **No data** is shared with third parties

## How the Extension Works

All analysis happens **locally in your browser**. The extension:

1. Reads publicly available data from the Chess.com and Lichess APIs (player profiles, game histories, and ratings that are already visible to anyone)
2. Processes this data entirely within your browser to calculate risk scores
3. Displays results in an overlay on the chess website you are currently using

No data ever leaves your browser. No data is sent to any server operated by us or any third party.

## Permissions

The extension requests the following browser permissions:

- **storage**: To save your preferences (auto-show overlay, rated games only) locally in your browser
- **activeTab**: To interact with Chess.com and Lichess pages you are currently viewing
- **Host permissions** (chess.com, lichess.org): To access publicly available API data from these chess platforms

## Third-Party APIs

The extension makes requests to:

- **Chess.com Public API** (api.chess.com) — to retrieve publicly available player data
- **Lichess API** (lichess.org/api) — to retrieve publicly available player data

These are the same APIs available to any web browser. No authentication or special access is used.
